Benefits (Ghee & Cow Dung Products)

Ghee ‚Äî Nutrient Dense & Traditional

Traditional ghee is rich in short- and medium-chain fatty acids, and contains fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K). It's valued for high smoke point and traditional use in Ayurveda and Indian cooking.

Cow Dung ‚Äî Agricultural & Household Uses

Cow-dung and composted manure improve soil structure and fertility and are widely used as organic fertilizer and in bioenergy/biogas applications. Dung-based products such as cow-dung cakes remain useful for rural cooking/heating and ritual use.
